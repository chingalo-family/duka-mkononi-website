Privacy Policy

history

Last updated: January 15, 2024

Welcome to the Duka Mkononi Privacy Policy. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our shop management services via our web and mobile applications.

1

General Overview

Duka Mkononi is a service aimed at helping small and medium-sized businesses manage their inventory, sales, and customers efficiently. When we say "we", "us", or "our", we mean the Duka Mkononi team.

By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will not share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

2

Information We Collect

To provide the best service, we collect various types of information through our secure systems:

account_circle

  • Phone number for communication
  • Account authentication details

  • Business name and location
  • Product lists and pricing

  • System usage statistics
payments

  • Transaction records for fees

Your information security is our number one priority. We use modern encryption technology (End-to-end Encryption) to protect your data against unauthorized access at all times.
