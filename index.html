About Duka Mkononi

Duka Mkononi, which means "Shop in Hand" in Swahili, is a mobile application designed to empower small business owners across East Africa and beyond. Our mission is to provide accessible, affordable, and powerful business management tools to entrepreneurs who are the backbone of their communities.

Built with modern technologies and a deep understanding of local business needs, Duka Mkononi helps shop owners move from traditional paper-based record keeping to digital management, enabling better decision-making and business growth.
