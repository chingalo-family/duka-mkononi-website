About Duka Mkononi

Duka Mkononi (Shop in Hand) is Tanzania's first comprehensive SaaS platform designed specifically for small and medium retail businesses. Starting in Dar es Salaam and expanding across Tanzania, we're empowering entrepreneurs with professional-grade tools that were previously only available to large enterprises.

Our offline-first architecture with cloud synchronization ensures your business never stops, even with unreliable internet. Built with Flutter for both Android and iOS, we combine modern technology with deep understanding of East African business needs, helping shop owners transition from paper-based record keeping to data-driven decision making.
